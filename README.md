# Blokus

[Blokus] (https://boardgamegeek.com/boardgame/2453/blokus) is a strategy-based abstract board game designed for 2 players. The goal of the game is to place as many of your pieces on the board as possible while blocking your opponents from doing the same. Players must place their first piece in the corner of the board. The only caveat to placing a piece is that it may not lie adjacent to your other pieces, but instead must be placed touching at least one corner of your pieces already on the board. When the game ends, each player’s score is determined by counting how many squares are left in their pieces.
